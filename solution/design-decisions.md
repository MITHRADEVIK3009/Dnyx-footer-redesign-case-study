# Design Decisions

| Decision                            | Rationale                                      |
|-------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Limited to 3 refined options        | Respected stakeholder time and decision bandwidth |
| Four logical columns                | Services → Company → Legal → Contact           |
| Prominent phone & Chennai location  | Immediate credibility for local clients        |
| Privacy Policy & Terms at eye level | Compliance + trust                             |
| High-contrast golden accents        | Full alignment with existing brand theme       |
| Reduced total number of links       | Lower cognitive load                           |
| Increased vertical spacing          | Better mobile experience                      |
